htmlapp

htmlapp is a new approach for developing web and mobile apps. It can be though of as a new type of CMS for apps. We are currently in development mode, stay tuned.

The goal with htmlapp is to make it possible to develop web and mobile apps without leaving the browser! The plan is to provide htmlapp as a service.

htmlapp is built with open source components and is available at Github. You can also test it right now, just open the developer console.
